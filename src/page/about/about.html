Cyberbullying


What is cyberbullying?


Cyberbullying is violence committed against someone through the Internet or other technologies related to the related to the virtual world.
Sendo a acção com o objectivo de assalto, perseguir, ridicularizar e/ou assediar.


The Canadian researcher, Bill Belsey
was the first person to cite and define
the word "Cyberbullying" in the world.

According to Belsey, it involves using information
and communication with technology
to harass a group or individual
in a deliberate and repeated manner.

Cyber = short for "cybernetic"
(something or place that has advanced technology).
Bullying = originates from the English word "bully
(which means bully, quarrelsome)
