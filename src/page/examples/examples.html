Cyberbulliyng


Examples



A case in point is that of Megan Meyer.
In October 2006, the American teenager
received a message from her virtual boyfriend,
a young man named Josh Evans, saying that
"the world would be better if you didn't exist".
The relationship that began on MySpace
(a social network popular between 2003 and 2005)
lasted only a month until "Josh"
began to attack Megan with words.
The next day, other young people began
to join the fight and also insulted Megan.
The teenager left the computer and went to her room.
Fifteen minutes later, her mother found her dead.
She had committed suicide.
